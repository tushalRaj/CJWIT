CULTURE IS… the natural outgrowth of a societies worship.

The word culture speaks of what has been cultivated (developed, nurtured, promoted, and advanced) in society. The root word of culture is cult – which speaks of worship. “So culture is the natural outgrowth of what a society worships. It is the out growth of a nations cult.” (Alan DiDio)

What we worship (or hold to be the essence of our life) determines our culture.

“What happens when a cult worships itself… the worship of man… at the expense of others. Then we stop asking what we can do for our country and we start asking what our country can do for us, at the expense of others. ” (Alan Didio) The worship of self tends towards tyranny, communism, socialism, and secular humanism. The worship of God tends to liberty and freedom. “Now… where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is liberty.” (2 Cor. 3:17b)

Whatever we behold is what we become like.

So my intention is to help you express images of impeccability, immortality, eternality in your personal and professional life, so when people experience your brands they experience a transformation in the beholding of your glorious solutions to their problems… the ones you solve with and for them. Why? Because culture is your enduring advantage. Because as venture capitalist Peter Thiel says “Everything you do can be knocked-off, the only long-term competitive advantage is your company culture.”

1 I’m Fanatical about Freedom.

FREEDOM is… the ability to pursue, that which is good, without impairing anyone’s ability to do the same.

There are 5 Freedoms:

1. SPIRITUAL FREEDOM

The ability to choose your thoughts, attitudes, and responses. This can never be forcefully taken from you.

2. PHYSICAL FREEDOM

The ability to have your body to get you through adversity and to the: fulfillment of your purpose, the bringing to pass of your vision, and the accomplishment of your mission.

3. FINANCIAL FREEDOM

The ability to get your passive income to far exceed your desired lifestyle and all the while you’re far from the fear of losing it all because you’re confident if need be… you could make it all back again.

4. TIME FREEDOM

The ability to set your own schedule. Imagine not having to wake up to an alarm clock if you don’t want to.

5. MOBILITY FREEDOM

The ability to go where you want. Imagine being able to go into all the world, at will.

The 5 Freedoms

There are ONLY 2 ways to lose your freedom.

1. FORCE.

It can be taken from you by an overpowering force. Spiritual Freedom is the only freedom that can never be taken from you by force.

2. TRADE.

It can be traded for something you deem to be of greater value. For example, a tyrannical trick is to get you to trade your freedoms for a “promise” of security and comfort.
